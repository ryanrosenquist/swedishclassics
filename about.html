A little about me

This site was created by Ryan Rosenquist, with a deep love of Swedish home cooking and a passion for food that has grown throughout his life. Ryan was born and raised in the USA, but from the start had a strong connection to Sweden through his Swedish father. Growing up in the 1990s, food became a way to keep Swedish culture alive at home – meatballs, pancakes, pea soup and other classics were often made in the kitchen and became a natural part of everyday life.

When Ryan later moved to Sweden his interest deepened further. What began as childhood memories developed into many years of experience cooking Swedish home-style dishes at home, focusing on tradition, simple ingredients and genuine joy in food. This site is a way to share that passion – to inspire, preserve classic flavors and show that Swedish food is both simple, comforting and full of heart.
